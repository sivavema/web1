About Us

 

Malaviya National Institute of Technology is a centre for imparting technical education of international standards and conduct research at the cutting edge of technology to meet the current and future challenges of technological development. Blitzschlag is the annual techno-cultural fest of MNIT. It has a long successful history over the past decade. Blitz boasts to be one of the biggest college fests of Rajasthan, attracting students from all over North India. It gives students opportunity to participate in a wide range of events and competitions both technical and cultural.Blitzschlag expands its horizons with each coming years achieving greater heights and success. Blitzschlag has not only spread its wings into many existing diverse technical genres, but has also given flight to many new innovative ones. These range from bringing in the renowned speakers from all across the nation to motivate today’s youth. Blitz is thus synonymous to a wholesome festival encompassing the everyday segment of life, work, passion in a single event. Being a youth festival, Blitzschlag also aims to target youth with social initiatives like blood donation drive, Anti-Depression drive; Blitzschlag makes it a point to make out a positive change in the society. Looking forward for your association with Blitzschlag, MNIT.
